Fourth year. The drive is almost meditative at this point.

Didn't do much this time. Sat by the Tungabhadra for most of Saturday. Read half a book. Watched a local fisherman work the same stretch of water for two hours with complete focus. Ate at the same place I've eaten at for four years.

The whole point of this trip is to have no point. It keeps working.
