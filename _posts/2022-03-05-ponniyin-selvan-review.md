## One line review - Ponniyin Selvan

Took two months to get through all five parts and it was completely worth it. Kalki built something extraordinary here. The scale of the story, the court intrigue, the way characters feel genuinely human across a thousand years of distance. Vandiyathevan might be the most fun protagonist I've read in a while.

Next up, Surely You're Joking Mr. Feynman. Classic I somehow never got to.
