## One line review - The Ministry for the Future

Kim Stanley Robinson writes about climate change the way nobody else does - with actual policy, economics, and the grinding institutional work of trying to change large systems. It's not a thriller, not a disaster narrative, it's something more like a systems analysis with characters attached. Dense, occasionally frustrating, and the most honest fiction I've read about where we might actually be going.
