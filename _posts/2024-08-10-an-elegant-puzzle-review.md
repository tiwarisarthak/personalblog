## One line review - An Elegant Puzzle

Will Larson writes about engineering management the way someone who has actually done it writes about it - with the specific awkward situations intact, not sanitized. The sections on systems thinking for organizations and on managing technical debt at the team level are the most useful things I've read on those topics. Dense with ideas, worth reading slowly.
