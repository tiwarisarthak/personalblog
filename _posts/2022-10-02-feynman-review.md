## One line review - Surely You're Joking, Mr. Feynman

Already knew some of the stories from the internet but reading them in sequence paints a picture of someone who just couldn't stop being curious about everything. The safe-cracking, the bongo drums, the strip club physics papers. What comes through most is that he treated every problem - hard or trivial - with the same genuine interest. That's the part worth stealing.
